Enterprise Agents

Agents with enterprise roles - drive projects, lead products, facilitate collaboration.

Enterprise Roles

🚗

Project Driver

Leads and drives projects to completion. delegates tasks, approves work.

DelegatesApprovesTeam: 5+
🎯

Product Lead

Owns product direction and roadmap. Makes product decisions.

DelegatesApprovesOwns Product
👥

Group Admin

Drives work through collaboration and engagement. No hierarchy - facilitates teamwork.

CollaboratesFacilitatesEngages
👤

Employee Assistant

Supports employees with tasks. Answers questions, helps with work.

Assists1:1Collaborates

Create Enterprise Agents

from orchestrator import create_agent

# Project Driver
driver = create_agent("project-alpha", "project_driver", config={"team_size": 5})

# Product Lead
product = create_agent("product-x", "product_lead", config={"owns": ["roadmap"]})

# Group Admin - collaboration focused
admin = create_agent("team-admin", "group_admin", config={"engages_with": ["team-a", "team-b"]})

# Employee Assistant
assistant = create_agent("assistant-jane", "assistant", config={"reports_to": "team-lead"})
